meatballs recipe

decription

this recipe is easy to make and takes a short time

ingredients

  • minced meat
  • parsley
  • oregano
  • spices

steps

  1. mix minced meat with all spices
  2. roll into sizeable formats
  3. heat pan, add a little oil or butter
  4. modertaly heat the meat balls
  5. turn until ready on all sides
